about us

Welcome to Opto vision

Opto vision is known for specialized services in eye care field since 2010. We offer a complete range of vision care services including comprehensive eye examination, vision therapy, speciality contact lens, custom made artificial eye, low vision aids, crutch spectacles, spectacle dispensing and many more services. We are optometrist and ocularist expert in vision care and artificial eye maker, having well equipped clinic. Please feel free with any questions or schedule an appointment.
